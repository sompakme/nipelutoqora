In A Nutshell Kurzgesagt Biography And Net Worth

Kurzgesagt is a German animation studio with a YouTube channel that focuses on minimalist animated educational content, using the flat design style. It discusses scientific, technological, political, philosophical, and psychological subjects.

In A Nutshell Kurzgesagt was founded by Philipp Dettmer in 2013 and currently has 14 million subscriptions and over 1.2 billion views since its establishment.

Aside from their German channel, In A Nutshell Kurzgesagt began creating videos for their Spanish channel En Pocas Palabras – Kurzgesagt at the end of 2019.

While their English channel mostly finances itself with donations from their viewers and individual sponsorships, the Spanish channel is sponsored by Wix, in addition to a grant from the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.

In A Nutshell Kurzgesagt Net Worth

The studio has been successful in its business so far. In A Nutshell Kurzgesagt has made for itself an estimated net worth of a cool $4 million.
