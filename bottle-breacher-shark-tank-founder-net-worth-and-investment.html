Bottle Breacher Shark Tank Founder, Net Worth, and Investment

Bottle Breacher is a unique bottle opener that made waves on Season 6 of the entrepreneurial television show Shark Tank. Founded by Eli and Jen Crane, the company specializes in creating custom-engraved bottle openers made from .50-caliber bullets.

The appearance on Shark Tank was a game-changer for Bottle Breacher. It resulted in a deal with two of the Sharks, Mark Cuban and Kevin O’Leary, who invested $150,000 for a 20% stake in the company. This strategic partnership opened doors, accelerated growth, and solidified Bottle Breacher’s position in the market.

The Story behind Bottle Breacher

The idea for Bottle Breacher was conceived by Eli Crane, a former Navy SEAL, while he was still serving in the military. He wanted to create a unique and functional product that would pay tribute to his military background. Using decommissioned .50 caliber bullets, he began handcrafting bottle openers as a hobby, which received positive feedback from friends and family. This led Eli and Jen Crane to establish Bottle Breacher as a business, operating out of their one-car garage. They sold their product online, primarily to the military community and gun enthusiasts.

Inspiration Strikes

While serving in the military, Eli Crane envisioned a product that would combine his love for craftsmanship and his dedication to military service. The concept of transforming decommissioned .50 caliber bullets into functional bottle openers took hold, and he set out to bring this unique idea to life.

“I wanted to create something that both celebrated our military heritage and provided a practical tool for beer enthusiasts.”

Eli Crane, Founder of Bottle Breacher

From Hobby to Business

What began as a hobby in their one-car garage quickly grew into a full-fledged business for Eli and Jen Crane. They recognized the potential of their handcrafted bottle openers and decided to take Bottle Breacher to the next level. With a strong online presence, the company started catering primarily to the military community and gun enthusiasts who appreciated the craftsmanship and patriotism behind each bottle opener.

A Commitment to Quality

Eli and Jen Crane prioritized the quality of their products, ensuring that each bottle opener was meticulously crafted to the highest standards. They wanted their customers to experience the uniqueness and functionality of their creations while also appreciating the attention to detail and craftsmanship that went into each piece.

The Appearance on Shark Tank

In Season 6, Episode 8 of Shark Tank, Eli and Jen Crane showcased their business, Bottle Breacher, to the panel of Sharks. They presented their unique bottle opener and highlighted its appeal to military and patriotic customers. The Sharks were impressed by the product’s quality, uniqueness, and the company’s commitment to supporting veterans and active-duty military personnel.

During their pitch, Eli and Jen asked for a $150,000 investment in exchange for a 10% equity stake in Bottle Breacher, valuing the company at $1.5 million. Mark Cuban and Kevin O’Leary were particularly interested and made a joint offer of $150,000 for a 20% stake in the company, which the Cranes accepted. This partnership with two prominent Sharks would prove invaluable in the growth and success of Bottle Breacher.

“We were thrilled to have the opportunity to present our product on Shark Tank. The positive response from the Sharks and their understanding of our mission to support veterans and active-duty military personnel was truly incredible. The investment and expertise from Mark Cuban and Kevin O’Leary will further propel our company forward.”

Bottle Breacher’s Commitment to Quality and Craftsmanship

What sets Bottle Breacher apart is their unique approach to manufacturing. Each Bottle Breacher is carefully crafted by active duty service members and veterans, who bring their passion and expertise to the production process. The manufacturing process behind every Bottle Breacher is a labor of love. It involves a series of intricate steps to ensure exceptional quality and precision. Starting with reloading, the bullet casing is cut and transformed into a functional bottle opener. The opener then goes through tumbling and cleaning processes to achieve a smooth and polished finish. Afterward, a powder coating is applied, adding durability and a stylish look.
