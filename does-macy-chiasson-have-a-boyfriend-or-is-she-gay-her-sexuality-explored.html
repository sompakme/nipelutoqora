Chiasson was bedridden for over three weeks recovering from the accident and chose mixed martial arts to get back the fitness level she once was when she was an athlete in high school. She then joined Krav Maga classes at a martial arts gym.

She eventually got attracted to the physical aggressiveness and the tough mental side of the sport. Macy then started teaching Krav Magna after one year of training. She transitioned to mixed martial arts and trained under Mushin MMA and Mid City MMA.

Does Macy Chiasson Have A Boyfriend?

Macy Chiasson is in a committed relationship with her girlfriend-turned-fiance Hannah Leffler, a UTA flute professor.

The love birds have been together for quite a long time. On July 8, 2022, Macy announced she is engaged to her long-term partner, Hannah. Both shared several happy pictures of themselves flaunting their engagement ring.

Macy captioned the post, “Every adventure we do it together.” Hannah, a Miyazawa flute performing artist, is available on Instagram under the username @hleffler22. With 677 posts, she enjoys a fan base of over 1.4k followers.

A dedicated teacher, Hannah, serves at the University of Texas at Arlington as the Senior Lecturer. She is equally committed to performing and has regularly performed with the Oklahoma City Philharmonic and the Fort Worth Symphony Orchestra.

She co-founded the electroacoustic duo WoodWired. The duo released their album, In the Loop, through UTA Records in 2018. Hannah serves as the Woodwind Department Head and flute professor at the Lutheran Summer Music.

Hannah has held flute faculty positions at the University of Northern Iowa, Luther College, Northeast Iowa School of Music, and numerous middle schools and high schools in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

Leffler attended the University of Northern Iowa and earned a Master’s Degree in Flute Performance. There she studied with Dr. Angeleita Floyd and worked as her Graduate Assistant. She then graduated with a bachelor’s Degree in Flute Performance from Oklahoma City University.
