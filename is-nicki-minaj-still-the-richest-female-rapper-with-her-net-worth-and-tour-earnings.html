Is Nicki Minaj Still The Richest Female Rapper With Her Net Worth And Tour Earnings?

Highlights

  • Nicki Minaj's diverse music skills and ventures across genres showcase her superstar status in the rap industry.
  • Despite controversies and personal life challenges, Minaj's career milestones and net worth solidify her success.
  • The rapper's savvy business acumen reflects in her endorsement deals, record label, and real estate investments.

Nicki Minaj's career trajectory in the rap industry has been triumphant, and her massive net worth is a solid testament. Minaj's journey in the industry started decades ago, during which she amassed a large and notoriously loyal fan base. The A-list rapper proved her expertise by extending her creativity beyond rap. Minaj is known for cutting across different music genres like R&B, hip-hop, and pop rap, thereby attaining attention from diverse audiences. With time, the Trinidadian rapper broke records as the richest female rapper and cemented her star power.

Over the years, Nicki Minaj has been shrouded in several controversies. From being entangled in feuds with fellow rappers to having a highly controversial marriage, the music star appeared embattled. However, she continued to thrive in her career with the unwavering support of her fan base, popularly known as the Barbs. She took her success a notch higher after going on her Pink Friday 2 World Tour, which sold out in several cities. This article will explore Minaj's net worth and tour earnings regarding her position as the wealthiest female rapper.

Inside Nicki Minaj's Pink Friday 2 Tour Earnings And Her Net Worth

Nicki Minaj started her Pink Friday 2 tour in March 2024 after announcements that the marathon concerts would be held across two continents and span 59 shows. In just a few weeks, the FTCU rapper made history as the female rapper with the highest-grossing tour, earning $34.9 million after 17 shows. Minaj's Pink Friday 2 tour faced a slew of scandals, but it thrived. This became one of her many major career milestones, including the title of the world's richest female rapper.

At $150 million, Nicki Minaj's net worth is the most significant wealth portfolio a female has ever attained in rap. The Super Bass rapper owes the bulk of her net worth to her chart-topping songs through the years. According to Billboard, Minaj has released three no.1 hits with 23 songs in the top ten singles chart. Her debut album, "Pink Friday," topped the Billboard charts in 2010 and made her the top-selling female artist in the same year.

Regarding her features, Yahoo reported that the multiple award-winner charges $250,000 each time she appears on another artist's project. Nicki Minaj launched her record label and revealed details about the business in 2023. Some of her artists are London Hill, Nana Fofie, and Rico Danna.

The star, known for being unapologetically outspoken, has also built her net worth through several endorsement deals throughout her career. Her highly lucrative gigs include Nokia, Pepsi, Beats By Dre, Fendi, Call of Duty, and MAC Cosmetics. Two years after dropping her first album, the Anaconda rapper aced a seven-figure deal with Pepsi, as reported by Forbes. In the same year, the star rapper launched her fragrance line, Pink Friday, which has since released nine fragrances. In 2013, Minaj earned $12 million as a judge on season 12 of "American Idol."

Her successful investment streak continued in 2015 when Jay-Z launched TIDAL. Nicki Minaj was announced as one of the stakeholders of the streaming platform. Per Business Insider, the "Starships" crooner owned a 3 percent stake in TIDAL. Despite amassing significant wealth for over a decade, Minaj did not invest in real estate until 2022. The rapper rented mansions in Beverly Hills and Calabasas, paying about $35,000 monthly.

However, in December 2022, Nicki Minaj bought her first home in Hidden Hills, California. The "Super Freaky Girl" rapper paid $19.5 million for the newly built 12-square-foot mansion before it landed on the market. Minaj also invested in automobiles, mostly painting her fleet in her signature pink color. According to VIP Fortunes, her array of car brands includes Ferrari, Lamborghini, Bentley, and Mercedes-Benz, among others. Minaj also owns a Gulfstream G5 private jet, estimated at $70 million.

How Nicki Minaj Built Her Career And Record-Breaking Net Worth

Born Onika Tanya Maraj, Nicki Minaj is the middle child in a family of five, who witnessed her dad's struggle with temper and drug addiction. Despite the dysfunctional state of her family, Minaj had a dream. Growing up, she wanted to become an actress and pursued an acting career until the 2000s. She pivoted into music and was discovered by fellow rap icon Lil Wayne.

Between 2007 and 2009, Nicki Minaj released three mixtapes that gave her a great start in the industry. She won the Female Artist of the Year award at the Underground Music Awards in 2008. Her debut album sold four million copies globally, and it was the beginning of her big wins. Nicki has since sold over 100 million records in her discography and won over 100 awards.
