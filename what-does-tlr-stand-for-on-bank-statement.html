What is a TLR transfer in banking?

About TLR transfer: The “TLR transfer” or “teller transfer” usually occurs when a check is clearing from your account. For a deposit, it again is referring to a teller transaction but this time is transferring funds into your account instead of out of it.

What does TLR cash withdrawal mean?

It is an abbreviation for an over the counter transaction performed with the teller. You would typically see this for a cash deposit or withdrawal done in the branch.
