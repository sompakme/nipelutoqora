10 Little Known Facts About Adele And Rich Paul's Relationship

Adele and Rich Paul have been dating for more than a year, and the couple has maintained a relatively private relationship, so here are some little-known facts about their romance.

Adele and Rich Paul have become one of Hollywood’s most dynamic power couples as each person has gained astronomical success in their respective careers. The couple who began their romance in 2021 had been single for a few years before deciding to date someone. Adele had been with Simon Konecki for eight years, and the couple got married in 2018 before separating a year later. After court proceedings, their divorce was finalized in March 2021.

On the other hand, Rich Paul was dating Jennifer Meyer, a jewelry designer and Tobey Maguire’s ex-wife, in 2018 before the couple parted ways. Their relationship was first speculated through various sources, and a few weeks later, they were publicly seen together at a highly-publicized NBA Finals game. As Adele and Rich Paul have a low-key romance that is going stronger than ever, let’s look at the little-known facts about their relationship.

10 Rich Paul Is A Successful Sports Agent

Hailing from Ohio, Rich Paul met a teenage LeBron James in 2002 and began his journey as a sports agent. He worked under Leon Rose before starting his agency Klutch Sports Group, in 2012, which has become one of the most popular sports agencies in the United States. His famous clients include Ben Simmons, Anthony Davis, Draymond Green, and LeBron James.

9 They First Met At A Dance Party

The couple took their relationship slow and had been friends years before they began dating. Adele, who makes rare appearances at events, first saw him at a common friend’s birthday party. They even danced together that night, and while she couldn’t remember the song, she guessed it was a track by Drake.

8 They Were Single Parents Before Dating

Both became parents at a very young age and, after having past relationships, stayed single parents for a long time. Adele has a 9-year-old son Angelo Adkins whom she shares with her ex-husband Simon Konecki. The ex-couple remain on excellent terms with each other. Rich Paul has three children, and his eldest daughter is in college.

7 Their First Outing Was At An NBA Game

Adele and Rich Paul made their first outing in July 2021 at the NBA Finals in Phoenix between Phoenix Suns and Milwaukee Bucks. Their appearance on the courtside sparked a conversation about the relationship, as Adele was rarely seen at a sports event. She was also seated alongside LeBron James, a friend, and client of Rich Paul.

6 They Became Instagram Official In September 2021

Two months after their first outing together and amid a myriad of speculations, Adele finally made her relationship official on Instagram in September by posting pictures from Anthony David’s wedding, a client of Paul’s. The black and white photo was taken inside a booth where the couple held each other close.

5 Adele First Discussed Her Relationship on The Oprah Conversation

While promoting her upcoming album 30 and her television special Adele One Night Only, Adele stopped by as a guest on Oprah Winfrey’s Apple TV+ show, The Oprah Conversation. The singer first spoke about her love for the sports agent. She gushed about feeling good and loving herself while seeing another person simultaneously. The singer claimed that her relationship was quite special.

4 Rich Helped Adele Get Out Of Her Comfort Zone

Adele has usually tried to control her public outings as she wanted to stay out of the limelight, avoiding going to places she liked. During her interview with Oprah, she admitted that Rich helped her return to her outgoing personality and attend events that she liked. She overcame her fear of how people perceived her because of him.

3 They Have Date Nights With Celebrity Couples

2022 brought a series of date nights for Adele and Rich Paul after making their relationship public. In January, they were spotted courts-side in Los Angeles watching an NBA Game and a few months later at an All-Star game. They also went to the celebrity-favorite restaurant Nobu with friend Anthony Davis and attended Kevin Love's and Kate Bock's wedding in June.

2 They Moved In Together In The Summer of 2022

Adele and Rich Paul took their relationship to the next level after moving in together in May 2022 into a brand new home. The singer posted a series of snaps on Instagram, including a picture with Paul, who is holding a pair of keys in his hand. They bought a $58 million mega-mansion owned by Sylvester Stallone.

1 They Hinted At Having A Family In The Future

After making their relationship public, Adele and Rich Paul have separately given rare interviews to promote their work, but interviewers have also asked them about the future of their relationship. Adele has detailed that she would want more children in the future after she can take some time off. Rich Paul also admitted that he deeply wanted to expand his family.

Adele admitted that she is utterly obsessed with her new beau, who has helped her grow as a person and supported her through the media scrutiny and pressure. The pair has just taken a big step by moving into a new home, and it won’t be surprising to see them getting engaged in the years.

Sources: Elle, Glamour, Harper’s Bazaar, People
