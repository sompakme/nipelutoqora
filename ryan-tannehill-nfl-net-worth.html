Ryan Tannehill is an American football player currently playing for Miami Dolphins as quarterback. His net worth is estimated to be $17 million.

Ryan Tannehill started his career when he became a part of the Big Spring High School's football team. He received the second-team District 4-4A honors during his stint with the team.

During his first season with the Texas A&M Aggies's football team, Tannehill was redshirted in 2007. Before he took the spot as quarterback in 2011, he played as a receiver for three seasons.

In 2012, Tannehill was chosen by the Miami Dolphins, 8th overall pick during the NFL Draft. He set a franchise record for the longest run by a quarterback with 31 yards in a single play.

Ryan Timothy Tannehill III was born on July 27, 1988 in Lubbock, Texas. He is married to Lauren Tannehill.
