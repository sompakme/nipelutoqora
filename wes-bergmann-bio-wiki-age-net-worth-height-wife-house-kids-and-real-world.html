Wes Bergmann Biography and Wiki

Wes Bergmann was born on September 10, 1984, as Weston Bergmann. He is an American entrepreneur and reality television personality who was a contestant from The Real World: Austin. Bergmann was a winner of The Duel and Rivals II, and a finalist on Fresh Meat, Rivals, Champs vs. Pros, Champs vs. Stars (2018), and War of the Worlds.

Bergmann also competed on The Ruins, Fresh Meat II, Battle of the Exes, Battle of the Seasons (2012), Battle of the Exes II, Rivals III, Champs vs. Stars (2017), War of the Worlds 2, and Total Madness.
