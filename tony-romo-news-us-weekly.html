Tony Romo News - Us Weekly

Name:Tony Romo

Hometown:Burlington, WI

Birthday:April 21, 1980

About

Born in Burlington, Wis., Tony Romo would later attend Eastern Illinois University and become the star of his college football team. In 2003, Romo was signed as an undrafted rookie free agent by the Dallas Cowboys and after proving his worth on the field, Romo assumed the starting quarterback role for the Cowboys during the 2006 season.

Romo is known for his good looks and gal pals as well as his skills on the field. The football honcho loves the Hollywood scene and has dated Carrie Underwood, Sophia Bush, and even has been linked to Britney Spears.

Romo began dating singer Jessica Simpson in November 2007, though he called off the relationship the night before her 29th birthday in July 2009. The NFL star popped the question to Candice Crawford in December 2010. They married a year later and share a son, Hawkins.
