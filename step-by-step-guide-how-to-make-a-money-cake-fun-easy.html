Step-by-Step Guide: How to Make a Money Cake Fun &amp; Easy!

Making a money cake is a creative and exciting way to celebrate a special occasion, and with our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to create your own fun and easy money cake. The cake is filled with money inside, creating a delightful surprise when it is cut open. Whether it’s a birthday, wedding, or any other memorable event, a money cake is sure to bring joy and excitement to the celebration.

  • Making a money cake is a unique and fun way to surprise someone on a special occasion.
  • Gather all the necessary supplies, including a cake, treat bags, money or paper notes, and a lollipop or stick.
  • Assemble the money roll using treat bags and a cardboard tube, and insert it into the cake.
  • Decorate the cake with frosting and add a lollipop or stick on top before serving.
  • When it’s time to reveal the surprise, have the recipient gently pull on the lollipop or stick to uncover the hidden money inside the cake.

The first step in making a money cake is to assemble the money roll, which will later be placed inside the cake for a delightful surprise. To do this, gather your treat bags and tape them together end to end. Insert money or paper notes into the treat bags and roll them tightly, starting from one end. This will create a long roll of money or notes that will fit inside the cardboard tube.

Next, slide the tightly rolled treat bags inside the cardboard tube. Make sure they are secure and won’t unravel. The tube will serve as the holder for the money roll, keeping it safe and hidden inside the cake. Once the money roll is placed inside the tube, you can move on to the next step in preparing the cake.

Now that the money roll is complete, it’s time to move on to the next steps in making your money cake. Prepare the Cake

Whether you choose to bake your own cake or opt for a store-bought one, preparing the cake is an important step in creating a stunning money cake for a birthday or any special occasion. Start by ensuring that the cake is at room temperature and ready for decorating.

If you decide to make your own cake, follow your favorite recipe and allow it to cool completely before moving forward. This will ensure that the cake is firm and easier to work with. If you prefer a store-bought cake, choose a flavor that complements the occasion and remove any packaging or plastic wrap.

Once you have your cake ready, use a sharp knife to cut a hole in the center, making sure not to remove too much cake. This will create space for the money tube to be inserted later. Remember to keep the cake/frosting scraps that are removed, as they will be used to cover the money tube.

To complete the preparation, carefully place the money tube inside the hole, ensuring that the money and lollipop are facing up. Cover the tube with the cake/frosting scraps, gently pressing them into place. Now, your cake is ready to be decorated and reveal the surprise inside!

Insert the Money Tube

The highlight of the money cake is the surprise inside, and in this section, we’ll guide you on how to insert the money tube into the cake to create an exciting reveal. Follow these simple steps:

  • Start by cutting a small hole in the cake where you want to place the money tube. Use a knife or a round cookie cutter to create a space large enough to accommodate the tube.
  • Once the hole is made, carefully remove some cake and frosting from the middle to create space for the money tube. Set aside the excess cake and frosting scraps, as you will use them later.
  • Now, it’s time to insert the money tube into the cake. Place the tube into the hole, making sure the money and lollipop are facing up. The tube should fit snugly in the cake.
  • To conceal the money tube, cover it with the cake and frosting scraps you set aside earlier. Press them gently around the tube to secure it in place and ensure a seamless appearance.

    • With the money tube securely inserted in the cake, you’re one step closer to surprising your loved ones with a delightful money cake!

    After all the hard work, it’s essential to store the money cake correctly and prepare for the ultimate surprise when it’s time to cut and serve the cake. To ensure the cake stays fresh and delicious, follow these simple steps:

  • First, cover the entire cake with plastic wrap or aluminum foil. This will help protect it from drying out and keep it moist.
  • Next, place the cake in the refrigerator. The cool temperature will help preserve the cake’s freshness and prevent any potential melting of the frosting or decorations, especially if it’s a warm day.
  • Avoid storing the cake near strong-smelling foods, as it can absorb odors. Keep it in a separate section of the refrigerator or use a cake storage container to maintain its integrity.
  • When it’s time to serve the cake, take it out of the refrigerator and allow it to come to room temperature for about 20-30 minutes. This will ensure the cake is easy to slice and enjoy.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    Here are some common questions that people have about making a money cake:

    1. Can I use coins instead of paper money?

    While it is possible to use coins in a money cake, it is generally recommended to use paper money or gift cards. Coins can be heavy and may cause the cake to collapse. Paper money or gift cards are lighter and easier to work with.

    2. Can I make a money cake for any occasion?

    Absolutely! Money cakes are versatile and can be customized for any occasion. Whether it’s a birthday, wedding, graduation, or any other special event, a money cake is sure to surprise and delight the recipient.

    3. How much money should I put inside the cake?

    The amount of money you put inside the cake is entirely up to you. You can include a few bills or go all out and fill it with a substantial amount. Just make sure that the money is clean, crisp, and in good condition.

    4. Can I use different decorations for the money cake?

    Definitely! You can get creative with the decorations for your money cake. Consider adding edible decorations, such as sprinkles or fondant shapes, to enhance the overall look. You can also personalize the cake by using themed decorations that reflect the recipient’s interests or hobbies.

    5. How should I store the money cake?

    Once the money cake is assembled, it should be stored in a cool, dry place to prevent the frosting from melting or the cake from becoming stale. If the weather is warm, you may need to refrigerate the cake, but be sure to remove it from the fridge at least an hour before serving to allow it to come to room temperature.

    FAQ

    Q: Is it difficult to make a money cake?

    A: Not at all! Making a money cake is fun and easy. Just follow our step-by-step guide, and you’ll be able to create a delightful surprise for any special occasion.

    Q: What supplies do I need to make a money cake?

    A: To make a money cake, you’ll need a cake (homemade or store-bought), a cardboard tube, treat bags, money or paper notes, a lollipop or stick, ribbon or string, scissors, tape, and plastic wrap or aluminum foil.

    Q: How do I assemble the money roll for the cake?

    A: Start by inserting money or notes into treat bags, then tape them together end to end. Roll up the bags tightly, starting at one end, and place the roll into a cardboard tube.

    Q: How do I prepare the cake itself?

    A: Cut a hole in the cake and remove some cake/frosting from the middle to make space for the money tube. Place the money tube inside the hole, with the money and lollipop facing up, and cover the tube with the cake/frosting scraps.

    Q: How do I cover and decorate the money cake?

    A: If your cake is homemade, you can frost it. If it’s store-bought, simply cover the top with additional frosting or decorations. You can also insert a lollipop or stick into the top of the cake for added decoration.

    Q: How do I reveal the money inside the cake?

    A: Store the cake until ready to serve, and have the recipient gently pull on the lollipop or stick to reveal the money inside. It will be a delightful surprise for them!

    Q: Are there any additional tips or variations for making a money cake?

    A: Absolutely! You can explore different cake recipes and get creative with your decorations. The possibilities are endless, so let your imagination run wild.
