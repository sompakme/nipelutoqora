10 James And Oliver Phelps (Fred And George Weasley) - $1 Million

James and Oliver Phelps are British actors (and identical twins) born in February 1986.

They began acting in 2001, landing their first and most prominent role as the Weasley twins in the Harry Potter series.

The duo's unique ability to inject comic relief amid the dark plot twists endeared the audience to the mischievous pair.

Since the Harry Potter series concluded, the brothers have consistently worked together as a team on other film productions and projects. In 2009, they were cast in the TV series Kingdom.

James Phelps and Oliver Phelps reportedly have net worths of $1 million respectively.

9 Evanna Lynch (Luna Lovegood) - $4 Million

Evanna Lynch, an Irish actress, portrayed Luna Lovegood in the Harry Potter series, and Lynch one of the cast members Rowling calls the "Big Seven."

Following her debut in Harry Potter And The Order of the Phoenix, Lynch quickly became popular among fans. She captivated viewers and the producers with her lively portrayal of Luna's unconventional and highly eccentric character.

Empire Magazine lists Lynch as the eighth greatestHarry Potter Character. Leveraging her prominence from the series, she has been cast in numerous films, including GBF.

As of early 2024, Evanna Lynch's net worth is estimated to stand at $4 million.

8 Matthew Lewis (Neville Longbottom) - $10 Million

Matthew Lewis, who portrayed Neville Longbottom, is a British actor born in June 1989.

He made his debut appearance in the TV film Some Life, but Lewis gained prominence for his role in the Harry Potter series.

He turned in a steller performance as the intricate character of Neville.

As the series progressed, he took the audience on an exciting journey while unmasking Neville's bravery, which was initially concealed with humor. In 2016, he landed a supporting role in the blockbuster film Me Before You.

Matthew Lewis' net worth is currently estimated at $10 million.

7 Bonnie Wright (Ginny Weasley) - $12 To $15 Million

Bonnie Wright is a British actress and movie producer born in February 1991.

She landed her first professional role as Ginny Weasley in the Harry Potter series. Variety magazine lauded her performance in the series, describing her character as "pleasant."

Wright is also one of the cast members Rowling regards as the "Big Seven." She launched her film production enterprise in 2021, leveraging her prominence from the franchise.

Following her notable role in the Harry Potter films, Wright was nominated for a "Best Female Newcomer" award in 2012.

As of 2024, Bonnie Wright's net worth is estimated at between $12 million and $15 million.

6 Maggie Smith (Professor McGonagall) - $20 To $22 Million

Dame Maggie Smith is an accomplished British actress who has ingratiated herself into viewers' hearts through a verifiable track record of astounding performances.

Smith's well-established career and bevy of comic roles have left a lasting and endearing impression, which has gained her various awards and global fame.

She is recognized as one of Britain's most prominent actresses and has featured in outstanding film and theater performances, including Othello and Clash Of The Titans.

Her iconic portrayal of Professor McGonagall in the Harry Potter film series further solidified her place in the entertainment industry.

As of early 2024, Maggie Smith's net worth is estimated to be between $20 and $22 million.
