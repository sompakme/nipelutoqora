What You Should Know About Netflix's Luckiest Girl Alive, Starring Mila Kunis

Netflix’s latest drama film, Luckiest Girl Alive, has arrived on the platform. Like the book that serves as the source material, Netflix’s adaptation of Luckiest Girl Alive will follow Ani FaNelli (Mila Kunis), a witty, success-driven, and ambitious woman who holds a prestigious position at a prestigious fashion magazine. With a high-profile Nantucket wedding in the works, she's got it all. But, Ani's glamorous, meticulous life threatens to unravel when a documentary filmmaker asks her to share her story about a shocking crime that occurred during her high school years.

Mila Kunis has starred in some pretty popular movies including Jupiter Ascending alongside Channing Tatum, and Fantastic Beasts star, Eddie Redmayne. She’s also starred in ted, and Oz: The Great and Powerful to name a few. But, this is said to be the most demanding role of the Black Swan star’s career yet. Here’s what you should know about Netflix’s Luckiest Girl Alive.

8 Mila Kunis Produced Luckiest Girl Alive

In addition to being the star of Luckiest Girl Alive, which is definitely set to be one of her darkest performances to date. The expanding actress is confident about it, and she's also adding another producer credit to her resume. She’s previously produced on multiple projects including A Bad Moms Christmas (2017), and Stoner Cats (2021-2022).

7 Luckiest Girl Alive Is Based On A Novel Of The Same Name

According to the author of the book, Luckiest Girl Alive was a personal, soul-searching, and cathartic book. Her voice plays a crucial role in the novel's success. Thus, it makes sense Jessica Knoll would write the screenplay for Netflix's adaptation.

6 Who Are The Stars Of Luckiest Girl Alive?

Alongside Mila Kunis, Luckiest Girl Alive will feature actors like Finn Wittrock (American Horror Story), Scoot McNairy (Argo), Connie Britton (Promising Young Woman), Justine Lupe (Succession), and Chiara Aurelia (Fear Street: Part Two - 1978).

5 Luckiest Girl Alive Is The Latest Film From The Handmaid’s Tale Emmy-Nominated Director Mike Barker

Mike Barker is also credited as director on Luckiest Girl Alive.

With several high-profile projects under his belt, the Emmy-nominated director has proven his credentials. His work on Hulu's The Handmaid's Tale earned him two Emmy nominations. Barker has also directed on multiple TV series including Versailles, Z: The Beginning of Everything, Outlander, and Netflix’s The Sandman adaptation. He’s also directed big screen projects including A Good Woman, Shattered, and To Kill a King, however Luckiest Girl Alive is his first feature film since 2007.

4 It Took Six Years To Make Luckiest Girl Alive

While the source material was an instant hit when the book was released in 2015, like many adaptations, making it into a film has been a lengthy six-year process for the first-time screenwriter.

In February 2021, Knoll announced Mila Kunis' casting using a post on her Instagram account detailing the multiple drafts and, later, redrafts she went through. The movie arrived on Netflix, on October 7th.

3 Luckiest Girl Alive Was Filmed In New York City And Toronto

The movie was filmed in the summer of 2021. Daily Mail shared photos Kunis and Finn Wittrock filming a dramatic scene in New York City.

In addition, the screenwriter Jessica Knoll shared an instagram post about joining the team in Toronto, “I join team Luckiest Girl Alive in Toronto in one week!” She wrote.

2 Reese Witherspoon Almost Starred In Luckiest Girl Alive

In 2015, Variety reported that Reese Witherspoon was set to star in and produce Luckiest Girl Alive. However, for reasons that remain unknown, Witherspoon left the project. The actress’ production company has been working on a barrage of projects including Legally Blonde 3, and Tinkerbell, so it’s possible that she might have dropped out of the project due to a packed schedule.

1 What’s Next For Mila Kunis?

Mila Kunis is taking on more behind the scenes roles in her film/TV career. In addition to her already impressive resume, Kunis is once again stepping into the role of producer on two TV shows: Made In LA and Hunted.

Additionally, the actress who starred in That ‘70s Show from 1998 to 2006 is reprising her role as Jackie Burkhart for a sequel spinoff series called That ‘90s Show. The series, which will also see several cast members from its predecessor reprise their roles, is currently in post-production.
