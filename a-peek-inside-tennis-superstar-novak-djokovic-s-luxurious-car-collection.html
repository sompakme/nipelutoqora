A Peek Inside Tennis Superstar Novak Djokovic's Luxurious Car Collection

Novak Djokovic is unquestionably one of the best tennis players in the world, and his followers are just as fascinated by his passion for automobiles as they are by his natural ability in the sport of tennis. Novak Djokovic has earned a great fortune and since it is common knowledge that his car collection is exceptional, everyone is eager to get a better look at it. Novak Djokovic has been ranked number one for 332 straight weeks and has won the year-end tournaments six times. His life revolves mostly around sports, but in his spare time, he enjoys riding around on some amazing cars. Here is Novak Djokovic's luxurious car collection.

Peugeot e-208 x Novak Djokovic

 

Novak Djokovic, a tennis star from Serbia, is not only an extremely proud owner of Peugeot, but he is also an extremely proud marketing advocate for Peugeot. Because he won the Australian 2020 competition, the legendary tennis player was presented with a gift and a prize in the form of his very own limited edition e-208 from the company. It is a totally electric vehicle, the tennis player's childhood fantasy. They unveiled the vehicle following his victory and seek to excite electric-car enthusiasts by designing a vehicle with features that are contrary to the norm. Its 50 kWh lithium-ion battery and single-speed transmission are suitable for a driver who wants only comfort and ease.

Peugeot RCZ

 

The Peugeot RCZ is a concept-style sports coupe available in three fashionable trim levels. In Novak's collection is the Red Carbon trim, which offers the same features as the GT with the addition of clever modifications. The Red Carbon Peugeot RCZ, unlike the GT model, features an enhanced navigation system, metallic paintwork, and a striking red dashboard. The Red Carbon engine complements the GT. Both are 1.6-liter gasoline engines with 197 horsepower. The suspension is modified to deliver sharper turning and handling to match the increased power. Although the RCZ is a modest sports car, it is, to say the least, exciting and youthful, much like Novak's personality.

Bentley Continental GT

 

A Bentley Continental GT is one of the most spectacular luxury vehicles in Novak's portfolio. This Bentley model is among their most popular. The Continental GT's both look and perform well. In about 3.6 seconds, the 4-liter V8 engine propels the vehicle from 0 to 60 miles per hour. For a grand tourer that is generally driven leisurely, it is brisk and perfect for celebrities who enjoy driving in luxury. With a price tag of approximately $200,000, the automobile is one of Novak's priciest assets.

Mercedes Benz S500

 

The Mercedes-Benz S-Class was introduced in 1996 as a full-size sedan. It was a 4-door sedan at the time. Style-wise and performance-wise, the S-Class is now a genuine sedan. Clearly, Novak enjoys a classy and slow ride. The S-Class is quite popular among celebrities due to its comfort and luxurious appearance. The S-Class is equipped with a 3-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine and a 9-speed automated transmission by Mercedes-Benz. This makes it an all-wheel drive. Novak's ownership of an S-Class is unsurprising, given the vehicle's strength and complexity.

Aston Martin DB9 Volante

 

The Aston Martin DB9 Volante was unveiled for the first time in 2003, prompting widespread attention. Fortunately, Novak has one in his lineup. The luxury tourer is available as both a coupe and a convertible, with Novak's vehicle being the latter. Being one of the richest tennis players, you would assume he would choose the more expensive option, wouldn't you? Aston Martin has equipped their more recent DB9 Volantes with a 5.9-liter AM11 V12 engine, which received a facelift in response to drivers' need for an enhanced driving experience. The modifications were well worth it because the newer DB9 Volante can now reach 186 miles per hour.

Audi R8

 

Novak appears to have a passion for fast cars. The Audi R8 is the quickest vehicle in his collection, with a maximum speed of 205.1 miles per hour. The Audi two-seater supercar's V10 engine provides both speed and comfort. Surprisingly, a number of drivers claim that the Audi R8 is great if you're looking for both speed and comfort. Recent enhancements have resulted in a more aerodynamic driving experience, which is perfect for city driving and acceleration.

Tesla Model X

 

A Tesla Model X is another luxury car in Djokovic's collection. The Tesla Model X is an electric SUV that was released for the first time in 2015. Model X prices range from around $114,990 to $138,990 based on the selected options and upgrades. Djokovic's Tesla Model X is the performance-oriented P90D variant of the SUV. The P90D has a range of around 270 miles and can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in under 3.8 seconds.

Bentley GT Coupe

 

The Bentley GT Coupe is one of Djokovic's most exceptional car collections. The GT Coupe is a premium grand tourer that was introduced for the first time in 2003. It is propelled by a 6.0L W12 engine and is capable of 200 miles per hour. As with the other vehicles on this list, the GT Coupe's price ranges from approximately $202,500 to £315,000 based on customizations and upgrades.
