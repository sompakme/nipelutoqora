Benito Castro parents: Meet Arturo Castro &amp; Rosaura Hernndez

The television program Ventaneando announced that the famous television actor died at the age of 77 on Monday, September 11.

This Monday, September 11, 2023, the world of music and entertainment dressed in mourning upon receiving the devastating news that the beloved singer-songwriter Benito Castro lost his life, as Pati Chapoy announced in Ventaneando.

Arturo “Benito” Castro Hernández, known for his innate talent and his ability to convey emotions through his performances, earned a special place in the hearts of his fans throughout his career. His lyrics and his unique voice allowed him to connect meaningfully with audiences of all ages, making him a reference in the world of music and acting.

Through Ventaneando, Pati Chapoy confirmed the news during the broadcast this Monday, September 11. “Unfortunately we have just been informed that Benito Castro has just died,” she said.
Likewise, through his Facebook account, the actor’s nephew, Michel Castro Mazas, also confirmed the death of his uncle: “Unfortunate and sad news: my uncle Benito Castro, the last remaining member of my great family, has just passed away. Castro. We are once again in mourning and terribly dismayed that my dear and beloved uncle Benito Castro rest in the peace of God,” he wrote.

According to what his niece described to Ventaneando, the actor suffered a serious accident this Monday morning.

Benito Castro Hernández was a Mexican musician, singer, composer, actor, and comedian. He was born on June 5, 1946, in Mexico City, Mexico. He was known for being part of the musical group Los Hermanos Castro, along with his brothers. He was also a close friend of Paco Stanley, a renowned Mexican television host.

In addition to his career in music, Castro also ventured into acting, participating in films and television shows.

Benito Castro parents: Meet Arturo Castro &amp; Rosaura Hernández

Castro was born into a family of musicians and entertainers. His late father, Arturo Castro aka “El Bigoton” Castro, was a famous Mexican comedian/actor who appeared in numerous movies during the Gold Age of Mexican Cinema and as a headliner in nightclubs and theaters.

His mother, Rosa Hernandez de Castro, was a homemaker. He has two sisters. His first cousin is Gualberto Castro who is one of the original members of Los Hermanos Castro. His third cousin is Daniela Castro, a popular Mexican TV actress.
