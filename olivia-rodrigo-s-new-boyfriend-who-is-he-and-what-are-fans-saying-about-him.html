Olivia Rodrigo's New Boyfriend, Who Is He And What Are Fans Saying About Him?

Ever since the 2021 release of her song entitled "Driver’s License," the fascination with all things Olivia Rodrigo became huge. So, it makes sense that when news broke out that she had a NEW boyfriend, who isn't Joshua Bassett, the curiosity surrounding Rodrigo and her new beau grew even greater.

Olivia's became more popular in Hollywood once she was cast in Disney+’s High School Musical: The Musical: The Series. But what made her become the super star people know today was the release of her debut album entitled Sour, where the song “Driver’s License” is included. “Driver’s License” is a breakup song that her fans assume to be about Olivia Rodrigo’s HSM:TMTS co-lead Joshua Bassett.

Well, it appears that her nights of crying over her ex, whom she addressed as a traitor is over, which pleased Rodrigo’s fans. Not only that, but it looks like she has also moved on and found love once again this time with a man named Adam Faze.

Who is this new love interest of Olivia Rodrigo, and what are fans saying about him?

9 First Sighting Of The Couple

The couple was first seen together during the Space Jam 2 premiere which was held at Six Flags Magic Mountain just north of Los Angeles. They were making some public displays of affection and holding hands. E! News has confirmed that their relationship is official. Olivia presented Adam Faze as her boyfriend. They spoke with another attendee of Space Jam 2 who said the couple was cute and appeared to be very comfortable with each other.

8 How Long Have They Been Dating?

Olivia Rodrigo and Adam Faze have been keeping their relationship private. It was said that the two met years ago after some common friends from the industry introduced them. They have been friends for quite some time, but it appears things have changed in their relationship to become much more romantic.

7 Who Is Adam Faze?

Aside from being the new boyfriend of singer and actress Olivia Rodrigo, Adam Faze is a also producer. The 24-year-old currently resides in Los Angeles, California. Born into a Christian family, Faze was raised by his businessman father and housewife mother.

6 Adam Faze Is A Producer

Aside from producing for movies and television (Day by Day, River Fork, and How Moving) Faze also produces music videos. He is currently affiliated with Goody Grace and has also produced the music video for his song that features G-Eazy entitled Girls in the Suburbs Singing Smiths Songs.

5 He Is A Former Writer For 'Forbes' Magazine

Adam Faze previously worked as a former media content contributor for Forbes during his early 20’s in 2017. As seen on the Forbes site, his bio reads: “Adam Faze is a twenty-year-old storyteller and creative entrepreneur focused on one thing and one thing only: the future of entertainment. Adam is the co-founder of Chateau Savant, an event series that launched this year during the Cannes Film Festival (May 18-26, 2017), and is represented by Brillstein Entertainment Partners and Ziffren Brittenham LLP.”

4 Faze Works As A Director And Writer For Television

On Adam Faze’s IMDb page, his resume adds director and writer along with his known producer profession. The majority of his projects happened in 2020 and 2021, writing for the popular TV series called Day by Day. Likewise, he also directed another music video of Goody Grace entitled Nothing Good, which features the collaboration of Goody Grace, Juicy J and G-Eazy.

In partnership with Jamie Dolan, Faze established a production company, Must B Nice in March 2020. He even reflected on how he and Dolan started their company right when COVID-19 began, stating how they wondered about doing something about their current situation where their writers and actors are all at home, in quarantine. This gave birth to their narrative podcast entitled Day by Day, a bi-weekly show that aired April 17, 2020.

3 Adam Faze, Olivia Rodrigo And Their Social Media Privacy

Although Faze and Rodrigo’s sighting at Six Flags in June went viral, their relationship remains offline as of today. Faze has two Instagram accounts, and his personal account is private. However, his professional account @itsnotafaze is open to the public and is being followed by Rodrigo herself.

On the other hand, Olivia Rodrigo, on her account, has not yet posted anything regarding her relationship with Adam Faze.

2 Fans Mind The Gap

What are fans saying about Olivia Rodrigo’s new love interest, Adam Faze? Well, for starters, they are not really in total support of the relationship, as the age gap between the couple is six years. As Olivia just turned 18 last February 2021, Faze is believed to be 24-years-old.

This was also the reason why fans went crazy on social media, especially on Twitter. When news broke about the movie event in California, where they were seen holding hands and being together the entire night. Olivia Rodrigo’s diehard followers expressed their thoughts on the social media platform, stating their dislike of the new couple.

However, it was not because Adam Faze is not worthy or might prove to be bad for Olivia, but mainly because of the age disparity between the two.

1 Was He Dating Someone Just Before Olivia?

According to one Twitter user, Adam Faze might have actually been involved in another relationship just before getting together with the "Good 4 U" singer. And it would appear that he didn't end things amicably with the other girl, because she claims he totally ghosted her for Olivia, and it looks like she has the receipts to prove it.

However, fans of Olivia are coming to her defense stating she would not jump into another complicated relationship after just getting out of one.
