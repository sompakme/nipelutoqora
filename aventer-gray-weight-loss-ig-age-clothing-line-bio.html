Aventer Gray Bio

Aventer Gray is An American speaker, teacher, pastor, mentor, entrepreneur, and philanthropist.

Aventer was born on December 15, 1981, in Dothan, Alabama.

She has degrees in Cardiopulmonary Science and Health Service Administration.

Aventer serves as the co-pastor of Relentless Church in Greenville, South Carolina.

She is also the COO of John Gray Ministries.

Aventer co-starred in the hit docu-series, The Book of John Gray.

She is also the founder and president of the Reign Dance Company.

Aventer Gray is married to John Gray. John Gray is a pastor, speaker, mentor, entrepreneur, philanthropist, producer, and musical artist.

She and her husband John have two children; John W. Gray IV and Theory Aspyn-Sky Gray.
