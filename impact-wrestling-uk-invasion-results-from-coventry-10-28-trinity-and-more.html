IMPACT Wrestling UK Invasion Results From Coventry (10/28): Trinity And More

On October 28, IMPACT Wrestling continued their UK Invasion tour from HMV Empire in Coventry, England.

Trinity, Josh Alexander, and other stars competed at the show.

The results, courtesy of Cagematch, are as follows:

  • Frankie Kazarian def. Leon Slater
  • Emersyn Jayne & Jordynne Grace def. Dani Luna & Deonna Purrazzo
  • Rich Swann def. Amir Jordan
  • Brian Myers def. Grado
  • Moose def. Rhino
  • Eddie Edwards def. Joe Hendry
  • Trinity def. Gisele Shaw
  • Eric Young & Josh Alexander defeat Chris Sabin & Frankie Kazarian and Subculture (Flash Morgan Webster & Mark Andrews
