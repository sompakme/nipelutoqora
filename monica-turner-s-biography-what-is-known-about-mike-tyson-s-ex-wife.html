Relationship with Mike Tyson
According to the Sun report, Mike Tyson and Monica Turners met in 1990 at a party in New Jersey, United States, hosted by actor and comedian Eddie Murphy.
Tyson, at that time, was married to his first wife and actress, Robbin Givens, with their relationship coming to an end after one year.
After seeing each other a few more times, Monica and Tyson started dating and kept their relationship silent.
However, two years into their relationship, the Boxer was convicted for r*pe and was sentenced to six years in jail and four-year probation. He was sentenced and convicted in 1992 but was released in March 1995 after serving three years in prison.
While Tyson was in jail, Monica Turner would visit him at least every two weeks to check on him; this further helped build their bond.
However, on the 19th of April 1997, they got married in a quiet Muslim ceremony. 

Monica Turner’s children
She and the boxer had two children together, namely, Rayna Tyson, born on February 14, 1996, and Amir Tyson, born on August 6, 1997.

Rayna Tyson, who graduated from the prestigious New York University, where she pursued Film and Television Production, has gained ground in Hollywood as an actress. One of the movies she was involved in is the famous movie, Joker.
Amir Tyson finished from American University in Washington DC, where he pursued Broadcast Journalism with a minor in History. He is the CEO of Debonair Attire, a clothing line label.
Apart from Rayna and Amir, who she had for Tyson,  Monica Turner had a daughter named Gena, born in 1989 in her previous relationship with Eugene B. Byrd.

Divorce
A year after their marriage, the veteran boxer was again convicted. This time, he was accused of assaulting two motorists and was sentenced to one year in prison in August 1998 and was released in February 1999.
In 2000, Monica filed for divorce as she claimed that Tyson was unfaithful and had cheated on her.
She revealed the boxer rarely spent his time at home and was a womaniser and spendthrift spending most of his money on women, cars and other valuable possessions.
After three years of going back and forth, they were officially divorced in 2003. Monica was awarded $10 million as a divorce settlement, including a luxury estate in Farmington and the Bethesda Home, with the two houses reportedly worth $8.75 million.

Tyson’s other wife
Apart from Robbin Givens and Monica Turner, who the boxer once married and divorced, he married a third wife, Lakiha Spicer, in 2009, and they already have two children together named Milan and Morocco Tyson.
