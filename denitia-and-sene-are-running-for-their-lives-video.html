Denitia And Sene Are Running For Their Lives (Video)

Brooklyn based denitia and sene released an intricate, but straight-forward visual to their chilled-out, and motivating track titled, “running.” In the Aaronisnotcool-directed video, denitia finds herself attempting to run away from a dark past only to have her momentum halted by white masked men.

“running” is a song off the duo’s forthcoming EP, side fx, which is slated for a November 11th release.
