Yung Miami, DJ Akademiks Go Back And Forth, Bring Up Diddy Allegations

Yung Miami and DJ Akademiks came to virtual blows once again. The Off The Record host continued to goad the City Girls member into speaking on her ex-boyfriend Diddy following his sexual assault allegations.

The Caresha Please host was initially immersed in a back-and-forth with Armon Wiggins, a media personality. Wiggins suggested that Miami was angry at Akademiks and projected it onto him all while not speaking about her ex-boyfriend’s recent allegations, which prompted Ak to get involved.

“Dam … Shordy dissed me 3 months too late with a wack bar,” he wrote. “The blogs and ppl who use to jack her was roasting her. She don’t want no smoke wit me. Her rap career is done. We just wanna hear bout her getting peed on by Diddy. Has she been in dem freak offs or na??? @YungMiami305 I tried to give u rap advice u claimed I was hating. Babe I was right.”

Armon Wiggins and Yung Miami continued going back and forth, and DJ Akademiks added another quote tweet. “Don’t let her talk that money talk wit u,” he wrote. “Ni**a why she ain’t get on that wit me when I was violating. N**ga only way on earth she got more bread than BIG AK is if she got it by selling that 30 year old P*SSY to PUFF DADDY. A whole billionaire. @YungMiami305.”

Finally, she replied directly to the former Everyday Struggle host, and wrote, “Get in the field or STFU PUSSY NI**A!!!!” From there, she had a series of tweets bringing up Akademiks’ recent comments on allegedly having on sex with underaged women, specifically stating, “There’s no difference between 21 & 17.”

She brought up another old tweet from Akademiks, where he wrote, “Bhad Babbie got mad titties for a 15 year old” in 2018. “Stop talking to me about diddy bi**h talk to him big back bi**h you addressing me so I’m addressing YOU PERVERT A** BI**H YOU A F**KING CREEP!!!!!!!” she wrote. “You ain’t better than the next bi**h weirdo!!!! LETS TALK ABOUT YOU, YOU SICK BI**H!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!”

Yung Miami and DJ Akademiks have had an ongoing feud for years, and it got stirred up again when she dissed him in a snippet of a record that she previewed at a nightclub. “Akademiks, you a b**ch, why you speaking on women?” she rapped.

Ak had several disparaging comments about her former relationship with Diddy, claiming that she was his “side chick.” These comments heightened when he had a baby with another woman.
