The Concept Behind Boobie Bar

Boobie Bar lactation bars are specifically designed to support breastfeeding mothers in their milk production. The bars contain six galactagogues, including moringa, turmeric, Shatavari, fenugreek, flaxseed, and oats, which are known to enhance milk supply. Each bar is vegan, non-GMO, and free from common allergens. The ingredients used in Boobie Bar are carefully selected to provide a convenient and nutritious option for breastfeeding moms.

Wendy Colson – The Founder of Boobie Bar

Meet Wendy Colson, the brilliant mind behind Boobie Bar and a true advocate for breastfeeding mothers. With over two decades of experience as a registered nurse and board-certified lactation consultant in perinatal and neonatal nursing, Wendy brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to her work.

Driven by her personal experience as a mother and her dedication to helping breastfeeding moms, Wendy conceived the idea of Boobie Bar. Recognizing the need for a convenient and nutritious product to support lactation, she set out to create a solution that would empower and nourish breastfeeding mothers.

Wendy’s passion for supporting breastfeeding mothers led her to develop Boobie Bar lactation bars. These bars are carefully formulated with six galactagogues, including moringa, turmeric, Shatavari, fenugreek, flaxseed, and oats, known for their milk-boosting properties. The result is a delicious, convenient, and effective solution that enhances milk supply while providing essential nutrients.

Wendy’s dedication to supporting breastfeeding mothers and her mission to provide them with a trusted and convenient tool for lactation support have made Boobie Bar a beloved brand in the market. Her expertise, combined with her personal journey as a mother, has shaped Boobie Bar into the successful and impactful brand it is today.

Boobie Bar’s Success on Shark Tank

During her Shark Tank pitch, Wendy Colson captivated the Sharks with Boobie Bar’s remarkable success and promising future. With an impressive track record, Boobie Bar had already generated over $770,000 in revenue, solidifying its position in the lactation bar market.

Wendy’s pitch highlighted Boobie Bar’s strong partnerships with major retailers like Walmart, Babies ‘R’ Us, and Buy Buy Baby. These collaborations have contributed to the brand’s widespread availability and increased visibility among breastfeeding mothers.

The Shark Tank exposure proved pivotal for Boobie Bar. It caught the attention of Daymond John, who recognized the brand’s potential and made a strategic investment of $150,000. This partnership not only infused capital into the business but also provided valuable guidance and support from an industry expert.

With the momentum gained from Shark Tank, Boobie Bar continued to expand its reach and solidify its position as a leader in the lactation industry. Its partnership with Daymond John and the subsequent publicity brought significant growth and opportunities for the brand.
