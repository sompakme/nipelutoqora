These Batman Actors Have The Most Fancy Garages In Real Life

Portraying Batman in a movie certainly has a lot of perks, aside from getting a hefty amount of paycheck for playing the part; you also get to drive Batmobile on set. The Caped Crusader has been portrayed by different actors over the last decades and most of them have done more than one Batman project. The fictional superhero Batman has had infamous Batmobile in every single movie and TV adaptation, listed below are some the cars that were owned by actors who have played Batman.

Adam West

The list would not be complete if Adam West is not in it, West has brought Batman into every television in the U.S. back in the 60s. The Batmobile that West has driven in the series is still being recognized as one of the coolest cars on TV and even movies. However in real life, West used to drive a Porsche Cayman however he already sold it years ago. He sold his 2007 Porsche Cayman for just $38,000 in 2016. The car only has about 16,060 miles on the odometer and was just driven about 3,000 miles driven in the past four years before it was sold.

Jensen Ackles

Jensen Ackles is a proud owner of a Chevy Impala. The Supernatural actor has revealed in some of his interviews that he will be keeping the Chevy Impala which was the car that the Winchester brothers drive when the show finally wraps up. The car has been an iconic part of the show Supernatural for a decade and a half and Ackles is definitely going to put his personal touch into it. The car is currently selling on the market for $50,000.

Ben Mckenzie

Ben Mckenzie is a proud owner of an Audi Q5. Audi Q5 is produced by the German luxury car manufacturer Audi since 2008. It is a series of compact luxury crossover SUV from Audi. The second generation of Q5 has debuted in 2016 which shares the platform of the Audi MLB Evo which corresponds with the B9 versions of the A5 as well as the A4. The Audi Q5 currently has a base price of $57,300.

George Clooney

George Clooney is one proud owner of Chevrolet Corvette V8 C1 convertible. It is definitely one the most valuable classic cars out there and it is more than just a classic car to Clooney. The car was bought by his father in 1958 and Clooney is doing a great job conserving the beauty of the car. The Ocean Eleven actor has done a lot of upgrades on the car including covering the seats with leather. According to the report by Daily Mail, the rare vintage car that Clooney owns can be bought for about $85,000 but it is unlikely that the car would ever go on the market any moment now.

Val Kilmer

Val Kilmer is a proud owner of '68 Pontiac GTO Convertible. The GTO convertible was first introduced in 1963 and it is considered to be the most responsible car for popularizing the muscle car in the world. The powerhouse of the vehicle has proven to be a hit that numerous other GM brands has started introducing a high-performance coupés of their own which includes Chevrolet Chevelle, and Buick Gran Sport and Oldsmobile 442. The Top Gun actor Val Kilmer has stated that he loved his GTO mobile but has to part ways with it last year. The car currently costs $101,400 at high retails.

Ben Affleck

Ben Affleck is one of the celebrities who joined the Tesla movement which was led by Elon Musk. Gone Girl star Ben Affleck currently owns a fully loaded Tesla Model S which costs around $120,000. Model S has the quickest acceleration compared to other electric vehicle in the market and it is built from the ground up. Model S has high-strength architecture with a floor-mounted battery pack which is an incredible occupant protection. It features an excellent handling and aerodynamics which allows a top speed of 163 miles per hour. It is definitely one of the coolest and safest cars one can buy.

Will Arnett

Will Arnett is a proud owner of a Lamborghini which was revealed on his commercial with AT&T Nokia. The car in the said commercial is actually a Murcielago SuperVeloce which is considered to be the ultimate Murcielago. However the real LSU Lamborghini does not have a tiger print like the one on the commercial, the real LSU Lambo is just a simple and plain car that is a the first generation Gallardo. The original price of the said Lamborghini model is $450,000.

Josh Hutcherson

Josh Hutcherson is a proud owner of 1965 Mustang Shelby Cobra. The 1965 Cobra has begun life at the Factory Five Racing which was currently owned by the The Hunger Games actor Josh Hutcherson. It is the ultimate dream car for those who want a pro-built piece without having to go through the hassle of actually tracking down the parts and just waiting for someone to finally bolt them together. The price for the 1965 Cobra car is at $1,050,000.
