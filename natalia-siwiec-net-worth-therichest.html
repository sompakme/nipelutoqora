Natalia Siwiec

Birthname Natalia Siwiec Birthdate April 7, 1989 Birthplace Walbrzych, Poland Height 5' 7" (170 cm) Weight 37 lbs (47 kg) Profession Model Net worth $4,000,000 Source of Wealth Modeling Nationality Poland Marital Status Single Children None

Born on April 7, 1989 in Walbrzych, Poland, Natalia Siwiec is a professional model with a net worth amounting to $4 million in 2012.

This Polish model began earning international attention in 2003 after she was crowned as Miss Lower Silesia. She was then chosen to represent Poland to the Miss Bikini of the Universe Pageant which was held in China. From that pageant, she brought home two awards which include the Miss Photo and Miss Bikini of China Gate.

After representing Poland to Miss International Tourism which was held in South Africa in 2006, she became a recipient of Miss International Tourism award. She has also appeared on some world renowned international publications like CKM, Esquire, Playboy and Maxim.
