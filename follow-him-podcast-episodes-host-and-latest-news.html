About FollowHim

The Follow Him Podcast, produced by Shannon Sorensen and sponsored by David & Verla Sorensen, aims to provide original and educational content for individuals studying The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints’ Come Follow Me course. The hosts, Dr. Hank Smith and John Bytheway, interview experts to make the study of the curriculum enjoyable and insightful. The podcast is suitable for all ages and offers resources for fresh, faithful, and fun study.

Meet The Hosts

Dr. Henry “Hank” Smith and John Bytheway are the hosts of the Follow Him Podcast. With their combined expertise as motivational speakers, podcast hosts, and personal development experts, they bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the show.

Dr. Henry “Hank” Smith

Dr. Hank Smith, an Assistant Professor at BYU, is widely recognized for his work in consulting companies and school districts. He has touched the lives of countless individuals through his inspirational speaking engagements and authored books. Dr. Smith’s ability to connect with audiences of all ages makes him a relatable and engaging host on the Follow Him Podcast.

John Bytheway

John Bytheway, a graduate of BYU and holder of a master’s degree in Religious Education, has been teaching the Book of Mormon at BYU since 1996. He is a renowned author of several books and CDs that have inspired many individuals on their personal development journeys. With his deep understanding of scripture and his gift for communication, John Bytheway adds a unique perspective to the Follow Him Podcast.

Dr. Hank Smith and John Bytheway’s passion for helping others grow and develop shines through in every episode of the Follow Him Podcast. Their genuine approach, combined with their knowledge and experience, makes the podcast a valuable resource for individuals seeking personal growth and spiritual insights.

Finding Jesus Christ in the Old Testament Book

“Finding Jesus Christ in the Old Testament” is a captivating book that delves into the profound connection between the Old Testament and the life of Jesus Christ. This remarkable resource features a collection of short and insightful quotes from esteemed Bible scholars, originally shared on the Follow Him podcast.

As you embark on a chronological journey through the Bible, this book highlights specific chapters and stories that shed light on the presence of Jesus Christ throughout the Old Testament. Through the wisdom of these scholars, you’ll gain a fresh perspective on the impact of His character on human trials and tribulations, as well as the significance of His teachings for our lives today.

“The Old Testament is like a treasure chest, filled with hidden gems that reveal the profound and divine nature of Jesus Christ. This collection of quotes beautifully captures the essence of His presence in the Old Testament, providing powerful insights and transformative perspectives.”

By exploring the pages of this book, you’ll discover the wonderful unity of God’s plan throughout both the Old and New Testaments. You’ll gain a deeper understanding of the connections between prophetic foreshadowings and their fulfillment in the life and mission of Jesus Christ.

Take the opportunity to embark on a personal study of the Old Testament, guided by these scholarly quotes. Let the profound revelations found within “Finding Jesus Christ in the Old Testament” uplift your faith, deepen your knowledge of scripture, and inspire a closer relationship with our Savior.
